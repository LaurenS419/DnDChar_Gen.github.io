About

This is a random D&D 5e character generator! You get to select if you want the alignment, class, and race generated (or you can provide the race for the stats), and then the stats are rolled. The skill values will be given (with the race bonuses accounted for) along with their corresponding modifiers. This generator can be used for solely stats, or almost a whole new character! Whether it be an easier way of setting up your game, a faster way to start campaigns, or a fun challenge of randomized characters, this generator is useful to everyone.
