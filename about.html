About

This is a random D&D 5e character generator! You get to choose if you want the alignment, class, and race randomly generated (you can opt out of any, but a race has to either be provided or generated), and then the stats are rolled. The skill values will be given (with the race bonuses accounted for) along with their corresponding modifiers. This generator can be used for just stats, or for creating almost a whole new character! Whether it be for an easier method of setting up your game, a faster way to start campaigns, or a fun challenge in randomizing characters, this generator is useful to everyone.
